Astrology — Straight Answers

  1. Astrology doesn't really tell you exactly what's gonna happen and when. Some people treat it that way, but in my experience it tells you about potentials — and the way those potentials manifest depends on you and your energetic health.
  2. Unfortunately, I'm not able to answer the question based on Life and Death matters as it's beyond human comprehension.
  3. Despite astrology, everything depends on your own decisions and actions, as well as interaction with your partner. If there are mutual and strong feelings for each other, there will be a great future for this relationship. Wish you the best!
  4. Despite the misleading opinion, astrology can't provide exact dates of whatever it would be. It's more showing the possibilities of things to happen in our lives based on the movement of astral bodies and energies.
  5. I want to mention in advance that astrology speaks of life lessons and predispositions. The purpose is also for you to integrate these lessons and create the life you want. So, the purpose of the information should be for you to become more empowered to create the love life you want.
  6. Natal chart doesn't provide exact information about who will come into your life (name, nationality, age) — it is more abstract information. Natal chart provides information about whom you attract, what is your love energy, when you might marry, and what is the atmosphere of your astrological love house.
